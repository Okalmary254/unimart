Our Story

Our Mission

To provide MNU students and staff with convenient access to quality products at affordable prices, while fostering a sense of community and supporting campus life.

Our Vision

To become the premier online shopping destination for the MNU community, known for exceptional service, quality products, and student-centric values.

How We Started

Founded in 2025 by a group of MNU students, our online store began as a small initiative to help fellow students access essential products without leaving campus. What started as a small WhatsApp group has grown into a full-fledged e-commerce platform serving thousands of students and staff.

We understand the unique needs of the campus community because we're part of it. Every product we stock, every service we offer, is chosen with the MNU community in mind.

From students, for students - This simple philosophy guides everything we do.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery times?

We deliver within 2-3 hours for orders placed before 4 PM. Next-day delivery is also available for orders placed after 4 PM. Free delivery on orders above Ksh 1000!

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, bank transfer, and cash on delivery.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day return policy for defective items. Simply bring the item to our campus office with your receipt, and we'll process your return immediately.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes! Show your valid student ID at delivery or use code STUDENT10 at checkout to get 10% off on all non-electronic items.
